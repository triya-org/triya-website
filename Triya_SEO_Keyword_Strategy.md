## What Triya actually is (positioning for all copy)
**"Transform any camera into a privacy-first, AI-powered security & analytics solution."** Triya adds real-time AI to a customer's **existing CCTV** (RTSP/ONVIF, any IP camera) тАФ **no camera replacement, no rip-and-replace.** Connect тЖТ Detect тЖТ Act. Available **cloud** (fastest start) or **on-premise/edge** (Triya Edge Box), same platform & AI library. Proof: 30-day pilot at a **Fortune 500 manufacturer**, 22 detection scenarios, on the customer's existing cameras.

### Core differentiators (our moat тАФ lead with these)
- **Camera-agnostic / works with existing CCTV** тАФ no rip-and-replace, no vendor lock-in.
- **Natural-language video search** (English & Arabic) тАФ "show me intrusions from yesterday." Rare among competitors.
- **Cloud *and* on-premise/edge** deployment тАФ privacy-first, data-sovereign option.
- **Custom AI modules** тАФ bespoke detection models delivered in weeks.

## Difficulty framing (global buyers тЖТ specificity & differentiation are our edge)
- **Generic head terms** (`video analytics`, `AI video analytics`, `CCTV analytics`) тЖТ incumbent-owned (Avigilon/Motorola, Genetec/BriefCam, Verkada). **Top-5 = 18тАУ30 mo**, needs authority. Build *toward* these.
- **Differentiator terms** (`camera-agnostic`, `on-premise`, `edge`, `AI video search`) тЖТ our moat, **winnable 6тАУ12 mo.** тЖР spearhead.
- **Capability / scenario long-tail** (`fire and smoke detection AI`, `PPE compliance monitoring`, `ANPR software`) тЖТ high intent, **top-5 = 3тАУ9 mo.** тЖР fastest wins.
- **Competitor-alternative** (`BriefCam alternative`) тЖТ high intent, very winnable.

The decisive lever for head terms = **authority** (deep content + backlinks + digital PR).
